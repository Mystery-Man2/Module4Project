About Us

When we opened in 1998, we had only one goal: To bring as many venues and events to Effingham as possible!

We have had big names like Johnny Cash, Twenty-One Pilots, and even the Illinois Fighting Illini call this humble arena their home!

We are proud to have stood as a shining example for our wonderful city for so long and we are still aiming to bring as many events to Effingham as we can!
